Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How does RoadBite work? ▼
RoadBite helps you find food vendors along your travel route. Simply enter your destination, and we'll show you available vendors on your path.
How do I place an order? ▼
Browse vendors on the map, select a vendor, choose your items, and place your order. You'll receive a notification when your order is ready for pickup.
Is there a delivery option? ▼
Currently, RoadBite focuses on pickup orders at vendor locations along your route. Delivery options may be available in the future.
How do payments work? ▼
You can pay using credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or cash at pickup. All online payments are secure and encrypted.
Can I cancel my order? ▼
Yes, you can cancel orders within 5 minutes of placing them. After that, please contact the vendor directly through the app.
How do I contact support? ▼
You can reach our support team through the app's chat feature, email us at support@roadbite.com, or call our hotline at 1-800-ROADBITE.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information and payment details.
